## Title
What It Feels Like to Have a World Inside Your Head

## Description
A personal essay about what it means to build and inhabit an imaginary world  not as escapism, but as a way of thinking.

Most people assume worldbuilding is for fantasy novels or video games. But for some of us, it's a cognitive architecture: a layered structure of concepts, rules, and relationships that we use to understand everything else.

This video walks through STRATA, the world inside my head, layer by layer  from the surface down through the infrastructure, geology, and deep substrate  and asks what it means to think in worlds instead of sentences.
